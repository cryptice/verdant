Storage of your gardening data and the database backend are located in the EU (Stockholm). Some of the Google services listed above may process data outside the EU in transit; these transfers rely on the EU–US Data Privacy Framework and on Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

5. Retention periods

  • Account & content — kept while your account exists. Deleting your account erases all associated gardens, beds, plants, photos and notes.
  • Voice recordings — discarded within seconds, immediately after transcription. The transcribed text may be retained as part of the action it triggered.
  • Server access logs — retained up to 30 days for operational and security purposes, then rotated out.

6. Your rights

As a data subject under the GDPR you have the following rights:

  • Access (Art. 15) — receive a copy of your personal data we hold.
  • Rectification (Art. 16) — correct data that is inaccurate or incomplete. Most fields can be edited directly inside the app.
  • Erasure / "right to be forgotten" (Art. 17) — delete your account and associated data.
  • Restriction (Art. 18) — limit how we use your data while a complaint or correction is pending.
  • Data portability (Art. 20) — receive your data in a machine-readable format. We will export your records as JSON on request.
  • Object (Art. 21) — object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Withdraw consent (Art. 7(3)) — for purposes based on consent (location, photos, voice). Withdrawal does not affect prior lawful processing.
  • Not be subject to automated decision-making (Art. 22) — we do not perform any profiling or automated decision-making that produces legal effects for you.

To exercise any of these rights, email erik@l2c.se. We respond within 30 days (Art. 12(3)), free of charge. We may need to verify your identity by matching your Google account to the request.

8. Automated decision-making and profiling

Verdant does not perform automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you (Art. 22 GDPR). The app generates planting reminders and task scheduling, but these are advisory and you remain in control of every action.
