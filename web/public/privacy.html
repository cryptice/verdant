Verdant — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 May 2026

Verdant ("the Service") is a personal gardening companion provided by Erik Lindblad ("we", "us"). The Service consists of a backend at verdantplanner.com, an Android application, and a web administration interface. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

Data we collect

  • Account identity (via Google Sign-In): your email address, display name, profile picture URL, and Google account ID. We receive these from Google when you sign in; we do not receive your Google password.
  • Content you create: gardens, beds, plants, species, harvests, tasks, notes, supply inventory, and similar records you enter yourself.
  • Location (optional): if you choose to capture the device's location when creating a garden, the resulting coordinates are stored with that garden record. We only read the location at the moment you tap the capture button; we do not track location continuously and do not collect background location.
  • Photos (optional): if you take a photo with the in-app camera feature, the image you capture is uploaded to your account and associated with the item you attached it to. We do not access existing photos on your device beyond what you explicitly pick.
  • Voice commands (optional): if you use the voice command feature, a short audio recording is sent to our backend, transcribed via Google's Gemini API, and immediately discarded. We do not store the audio after transcription.
  • Technical logs: standard server access logs (timestamps, request paths, response status, IP address) retained for operational and security purposes.

How we use your data

  • Provide the core Service: authenticate you, store and display the gardening data you enter, sync it across your devices.
  • Operate and secure the Service: diagnose errors, prevent abuse, comply with legal obligations.
  • We do not use your data for advertising and do not sell it.

Who we share data with

  • Google Cloud Platform — backend hosting, Cloud SQL (database), and Cloud Build. Data is processed in the EU (Stockholm region).
  • Google Sign-In — identity verification.
  • Google Gemini API — voice-command transcription (only when you use voice commands).
  • Google Maps SDK — map tiles inside the Android app.

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties.

Data retention

Records you create are kept for as long as your account exists. You may delete individual records at any time inside the app. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the address below.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict our processing of it. To exercise any of these rights, write to the contact address below; we will respond within 30 days.

Children

Verdant is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced inside the Service before they take effect. The "effective date" at the top of this page will always reflect the current version.
